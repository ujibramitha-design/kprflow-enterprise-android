# KPRFlow Enterprise - Business Plan

---

## 📋 **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

### **Company Overview**
**KPRFlow Enterprise** adalah solusi SaaS (Software as a Service) enterprise untuk manajemen KPR (Kredit Pemilikan Rumah) yang mengintegrasikan AI/ML capabilities, real-time monitoring, dan multi-role collaboration dalam satu platform terpadu.

### **Mission Statement**
"Transforming property financing with AI-driven insights and seamless collaboration to accelerate KPR processing and enhance customer experience."

### **Vision Statement**
"To become the leading AI-powered KPR management platform in Southeast Asia by 2028."
