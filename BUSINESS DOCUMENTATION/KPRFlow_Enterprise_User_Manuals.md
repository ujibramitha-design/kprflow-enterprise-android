### **Application Process**

#### **Step 1: New Application**
1. **Start Application**: Tap "Apply for KPR"
2. **Select Property**: Choose desired property/unit
3. **Enter Details**: Complete application form
4. **Review & Submit**: Verify information and submit

#### **Step 2: Document Upload**
1. **Required Documents**: 
   - KTP (Identity Card)
   - KK (Family Card)
   - Pay Slip (3 months)
   - Bank Statement (3 months)
   - Tax ID (NPWP)
2. **Upload Process**:
   - Tap "Upload Documents"
   - Select document type
   - Take photo or upload file
   - Verify quality and submit
3. **Document Status**:
   - ⏳ Pending: Uploaded, awaiting verification
   - ✅ Verified: Document approved
   - ❌ Rejected: Document needs resubmission

#### **Step 3: Application Tracking**
1. **Status Updates**:
   - 📝 Submitted: Application received
   - 🔍 Under Review: Documents being verified
   - 💰 Under Assessment: Financial evaluation
   - ✅ Approved: Application approved
   - 📋 Contract Ready: Contract preparation
   - 🏠 Disbursement: Fund disbursement

2. **Timeline View**:

### **Document Verification**

#### **Document Review Process**
1. **Access Document Queue**:
   - Navigate to "Document Review"
   - Filter by status/priority
   - Sort by submission date

2. **Document Verification**:
   - Open document for review
   - Verify authenticity
   - Check completeness
   - Validate compliance

3. **Approval Workflow**:
   - Approve: Document meets requirements
   - Reject: Document needs correction
   - Request Additional Info: Missing information

#### **Document Types & Requirements**

| Document | Requirements | Verification Steps |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **KTP** | Valid ID, Clear photo, Matches applicant | 1. Check validity<br>2. Verify photo<br>3. Match with application |
| **KK** | Family card, All members listed | 1. Verify family members<br>2. Check completeness<br>3. Validate relationships |
| **Pay Slip** | 3 months, Company name, Salary amount | 1. Verify employment<br>2. Check salary consistency<br>3. Validate company |
| **Bank Statement** | 3 months, Account holder name | 1. Verify account holder<br>2. Check transaction history<br>3. Validate balance |

#### **Quality Control**
1. **Image Quality**:
   - Minimum resolution: 300 DPI
   - No glare or shadows
   - All corners visible
   - Text clearly readable

2. **Data Accuracy**:
   - Name matches application
   - Dates are valid
   - Numbers are correct
   - Signatures present

3. **Compliance Check**:
   - Document not expired
   - Information consistent
   - Required fields complete
   - Format meets standards
